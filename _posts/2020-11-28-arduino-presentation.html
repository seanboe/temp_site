Over Thanksgiving break, I decided to put together a presentation displaying the basics of arduino programming for people in my Arduino Club who are new to this. The club was approved a mere month ago, so it's time to get things rolling!

This slideshow starts with the basics-what variables are + data types and what a byte is. It eventually progresses to structures and function definitions. I hope to also add some slides dedicated to pointers as well and maybe creating libraries. The presentation is really just meant to introduce club members to arduino programming, and I may add a recorded video of myself presenting this to the club as well.


Or, make a copy of the presentation here if you want it for yourself. I've set up animations on the presentation, so put it in "present" mode to see everything correctly!

Enjoy!
